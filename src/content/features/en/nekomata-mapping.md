Use your gamepad as a mouse and keyboard, customizable per app. An On / Off toggle, in OmniNav or Classic layout with adjustable cursor speed. When on, your customized profiles take over, while common apps run on ready-made mapping you can customize too. Nekomata weaves a charm for each app, remapping every XInput control to keyboard, mouse, or cursor actions, and can raise mapping priority with Nekomata Boost, add hold-to-repeat, and prevent double input. Open the editor from the OmniCharm widget.
